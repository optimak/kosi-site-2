Frequently Asked Questions

How much do commissions cost?

Pricing is personalized for every project. Since each piece of art is unique, the cost is determined by key factors like the complexity of the design, the final size, and any required commercial usage rights.

To ensure you get the best value, we provide a detailed quote only after reviewing your specific vision and project scope.

Ready to find out your investment? Please Contact Me to discuss your idea and receive a customized estimate.

How long do commissions take?

Timeline depends on complexity and my current workload. Simple portraits typically take 2-3 weeks, while complex album covers may take 3-4 weeks. Rush orders are available for an additional fee.

What formats do you provide?

All digital artwork is delivered in high-resolution PNG and JPEG formats. For print work, I also provide PDF files with appropriate bleed and crop marks.

Do you require payment upfront?

I typically request 50% payment upfront to begin work, with the remaining 50% due upon completion. For larger projects, we can discuss a payment schedule that works for both parties.

Do you offer revisions?

I include up to 3 rounds of revisions in my standard pricing. Additional revisions beyond this can be made for a small additional fee. Communication is key to ensuring you love the final result!

Can I use commissioned art commercially?

Commercial usage rights are included for album covers and commercial projects. For personal commissions, commercial licensing can be added for an additional fee.
